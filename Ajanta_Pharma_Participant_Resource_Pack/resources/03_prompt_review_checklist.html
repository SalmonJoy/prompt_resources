Prompt Review Checklist

Tick each item before running a prompt and again after receiving the response. Keep this page at your workstation.

Before running the prompt

  • Is the business objective clear?
  • Is the intended audience identified?
  • Is the relevant source information included?
  • Are the instructions specific?
  • Are assumptions controlled?
  • Are constraints clearly stated?
  • Is the output format defined?
  • Are quality criteria included?
  • Is the information safe and approved for upload?

After receiving the response

  • Did ChatGPT follow every instruction?
  • Did it add unsupported information?
  • Is important information missing?
  • Is the tone appropriate for the audience?
  • Are facts and assumptions clearly separated?
  • Does it require subject-matter review?
  • What follow-up prompt could improve it?
  • Has the final output been verified?
Remember
AI output is a draft until a human reviews it. Verify important facts, references and calculations, and keep final accountability with the human user.
